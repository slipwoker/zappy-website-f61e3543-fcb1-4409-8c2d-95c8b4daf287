From the Holy Land - to your home

There is a place in Israel where the water is quiet in the early morning, where the light touches the hills softly, and where faith once walked on the shore. The Sea of Galilee is not just a lake. It is where Jesus walked beside fishermen, where He called His first disciples, where He calmed the storm, where He multiplied bread and fish, and where He walked upon the water.

For Christians around the world, the Sea of Galilee is not simply geography — it is living Scripture. 

And along its shores lie small stones. 
Simple. Quiet. Unnoticed. 

But these stones have rested where miracles happened. They have touched the same waters that Jesus crossed. They belong to the land where faith became history.

We live here in Israel, not far from Bethlehem. And every time we visit the Sea of Galilee, we feel something sacred in its stillness. We began asking ourselves: what about the people who long to visit this land but cannot? The grandmother who dreams of Israel but is too old to travel. The family who prays for the Holy Land every Sunday. The young believer who wants something real — something touched by this land.

So we created something simple, but deeply meaningful. We carefully gather small stones from the shores of the Sea of Galilee and arrange them into handcrafted pieces designed for your home — bringingChristian gifts from Israelto hearts around the world who long to hold a piece of where Jesus walked.

From the Sea of Galilee — to your heart.

Our Signature Pieces

Two Sacred Pieces,
One Holy Connection

Hands Holding the Stones

Life-size sculpted hands, crafted with quiet reverence, gently cradle authentic stones gathered from the shores of the Sea of Galilee. A small cross crowns the stones above — a symbol of faith held carefully, of prayer made tangible, and of Christ's protection over all who believe.

These hands speak the language of trust. They remind us that we are held — by grace, by love, by the same hands that calmed the storm and walked upon the water. A meaningful Christian gift from Israel, made to rest in your home as a daily act of remembrance.

Angel Wings Holding the Stones

A smaller, delicate piece shaped as outstretched angel wings, their feathers sculpted with quiet precision, gently cradling the same sacred stones at their heart. This piece speaks of divine presence — of heaven watching over the home, of angels standing guard over those we love most.

Place it in an entryway, a bedroom, or beside a family photograph. Let it be a quiet reminder that guidance surrounds your household — that the same God who walked the shores of Galilee walks with your family still. A treasured piece of Holy Land religious home decor.

Each piece contains authentic stones carefully gathered from the shores of the Sea of Galilee — the very waters where Jesus walked, taught, and performed miracles. Choose between the two signature designs, each made to bring remembrance, peace, and sacred connection into everyday life.
