Handcrafted in the Holy Land

The Collection

Two signature handcrafted designs, each cradling authentic stones gathered from the shores of the Sea of Galilee — one of Christianity's most sacred places on earth. The first is a pair of life-size sculpted hands holding the stones, crowned with a cross. The second is a set of delicate angel wings gently cradling the same sacred stones. Both pieces carry within them a fragment of the land where Jesus walked, taught, and performed miracles.

These are not mass-made decorative objects. Each piece is shaped by faith, memory, and artisan care — crafted by hands that live near Bethlehem, not far from the shores they hold sacred. They are meaningful keepsakes, designed to bring the Holy Land into your home and into your heart, especially for those who long to visit but cannot make the journey. Unique Christian gifts from Israel, offered with reverence and love.

Sacred Meaning

What Each Piece Represents

Hands

Faith is not something we hold loosely — it is cradled with care, the way a father holds a child's hand in the dark. The sculpted hands in this piece embody that tenderness: a reminder that your prayers are received, that your trust in Christ is never misplaced, and that you are never asked to carry your burdens alone.

These hands speak of comfort in sorrow, of strength when we feel weak, of the quiet protection that comes from surrendering to a love greater than our own. They are hands that bless, hands that heal — and now, they rest in your home as a daily invitation to prayer.

Nestled within them, a small stone from the shores of the Sea of Galilee — the very water where Jesus called His fishermen, where faith became history. Let these hands remind you that you are held.

Angel Wings

Throughout Scripture, angels are messengers of guidance — sent to comfort the fearful, to announce the miraculous, and to stand watch over those whom God loves. The angel wings in this piece carry that same eternal promise: that you are not walking through life unseen, that heaven is aware of your family, and that divine presence is woven into the ordinary fabric of your days.

To place angel wings in your home is to welcome a blessing over every threshold. It is a declaration that peace dwells here — not the peace the world gives, but the deep, abiding peace that passes all understanding. It is a prayer made visible, a gift of grace that speaks without words.

Within the wings, the sacred stone rests like a promise: a tangible piece of the land where angels appeared to shepherds, where heaven touched earth, and where the Gospel was first proclaimed.

At the heart of every piece lies a small, quiet stone — gathered by hand from the shores of the Sea of Galilee, where Jesus walked, where He taught, and where miracles unfolded that changed the world forever. These authentic stones create a direct emotional link to the Holy Land: a sacred connection for every Christian who longs to stand where the Gospel lived and breathed. These are more thanChristian gifts from Israel— they are fragments of living Scripture, brought gently into your home.
