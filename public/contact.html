Common Questions

Are the stones authentic?

Yes — every stone is personally gathered from the shores of the Sea of Galilee, one of the most sacred sites in Christianity. These are the same waters where Jesus walked, taught, and performed miracles. Each piece carries a real fragment of that holy ground into your home.

Who are these pieces for?

These handcrafted pieces are for any home where faith is cherished. They make deeply meaningful gifts for parents, grandparents, and families — and are especially beloved for baptisms, Easter, and Christmas. They are also for the young believer, and for anyone who longs to hold something real from the Holy Land.

Where are the pieces made?

Each piece is handcrafted in Israel, not far from Bethlehem — the birthplace of Jesus. Our artisans live and work in this sacred land, and their care and reverence is present in every detail of the finished work. These are trueChristian gifts from Israel, made with intention and love.

What makes them meaningful?

Beyond their beauty, these pieces create a tangible connection to sacred geography and Christian memory. The stone you hold rested on the shore where faith became history. For those who cannot travel to the Holy Land, this is a way to touch it — to keep it close, every single day.

More than decor, each piece is created to bring remembrance, comfort, and faith into everyday life.

Sacred gifts rooted in the Holy Land — crafted with devotion, carried across generations.
